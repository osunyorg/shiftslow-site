From the 12 - 19 August 2023, we invite once again to Kunst in der Natur in the Viennese forests. A one week long of transformative degrowth education. We want to explore the following questions:

  • How to inspire for degrowth?
  • How to spread degrowth?
  • How to create spaces to practice degrowth?
  • How to educate about degrowth?

In the mornings there are in depth holistic workshops around each of the questions. In the afternoons, we invite for co-creation, wanna explore hosting a workshop on degrowth and related topics? Then this is your space! In the evenings we expect concerts, poetry nights, art performances, jam sessions and on the last night, a party to which local DJs come from the Viennese scene.
