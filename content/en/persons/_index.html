ShiftSlow is an association for the promotion of a just and colourful social-ecological transformation, founded in 2021 in Vienna, Austria. We provide critical education around the topics of post- or degrowth, development strategies, cultural change and a global socio-ecological transition towards a good life for all. The aim of the young association is to become a think and do tank dealing with the design of a livable and just future and creates high-quality educational content and formats directed towards this end. 

We are an international collective of young people from Spain, France, Italy, Germany, Austria, the Netherlands, UK and Ukraine. We are a trans- and interdisciplinary team united by our belief in social ecological transformation through experiential, experimental education and culture change.
